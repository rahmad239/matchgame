Directions

Memory or Match Maker (as we like to call it) is a card game designed to help improve memory. All cards start face down, at each turn a player is to turn two cards face up in an attempt to reveal two matching cards. The object of the game is to reveal all matches.

Match Making Twist

Unlike traditional match games, this one will require you to match power couples. This was an excersice for me of taking note of couple that have made in impact on me and the way I percieve my abilities as an individual and within a partnership. I am a sprouting developer and this game reminded me of the power we have as creators in our craft and the power of combinining with other powerful people. This game was FUN! My goal is to experience the excitement and love for learning coding as I am building my skill set in it.
